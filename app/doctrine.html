Core principle

Monatise trades from stewardship, patience, truth, discipline, risk control, and alignment.

Faith is not used as a signal to force trades. The Word of God is the foundation for character, wisdom, restraint, and obedience to process. A trade is valid only when structure, risk, timing, context, and confirmation agree.

Execution Covenant

Pre-trade filter
01 Count The Cost

Entry, invalidation, stop, size, risk, reward, liquidity target, session, and market state come before execution.

02 Use Just Weight

No fake confirmation, forced bias, revenge entry, emotional sizing, or manipulated analysis.

03 Prefer Waiting

No trade is better than a bad trade. Missing a move is acceptable; entering without confirmation is not.

04 Accept Invalidation

When price violates structure, the framework accepts truth, protects capital, and refuses ego-driven re-entry.

System rule

No trade is also obedience.

  • Reject unclear setups
  • Preserve the trader's peace and capital
  • Never treat faith as a guarantee of profit
  • Turn every outcome into feedback

Framework Output

When alignment fails

If stewardship, honest measurement, confirmation, session condition, market state, stop clarity, acceptable risk, or peace fails, the correct framework output is:

NO TRADE - WAIT FOR ALIGNMENT.
