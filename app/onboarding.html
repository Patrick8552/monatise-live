Money flow

Monatise does not custody trading funds. Your capital stays in your own exchange wallet.

The app gives you a grid-trading console, risk controls, and order automation. It does not hold your deposits, process trading withdrawals, or become the place where your market capital lives.

Plain answer

Where do withdrawals happen?

  • Withdraw trading funds from Hyperliquid or your wallet route
  • Cancel or stop Monatise before moving funds
  • Confirm no live grid orders are resting
  • Monatise subscription payments are separate from trading capital

Connect Checklist

Before live
  1. Create or prepare a wallet Use a wallet and Hyperliquid flow you control. Keep seed phrases and private keys outside Monatise.
  2. Fund the venue directly Deposit through Hyperliquid-supported routes. Monatise should not receive trading deposits.
  3. Create an API wallet Use a trading API wallet for execution. Avoid pasting primary wallet secrets into web apps.
  4. Save credentials in the desk Log in, add account address and API wallet secret, then confirm the dashboard reports credentials saved.
  5. Start tiny Use the default small order clip, watch exchange order count, and stop immediately if sync looks wrong.

Withdrawal Workflow

Do this first
Stop the desk

Press Stop and wait for the Runtime Log to show the loop is no longer placing or refreshing orders.

Check open orders

Confirm Live Orders is empty or cancel resting orders at the venue before removing funds.

Withdraw at Hyperliquid

Use Hyperliquid or your connected wallet route. Monatise cannot release funds it never custodies.

Return carefully

After balances change, refresh the desk and let the risk console recalculate before restarting live trading.

Quick FAQ

Trust basics
Can I use Monatise without Hyperliquid?

Yes. You can learn, inspect live market prices, and use strategy preview without connecting a funded account.

Can Monatise withdraw my trading funds?

No. Monatise is designed as non-custodial software. Withdrawals happen from the venue or wallet where your funds are held.

What does Pro or Business pay for?

Subscription access pays for the software layer. It is separate from your trading capital, exchange balances, and withdrawals.

What should I do before funding bigger size?

Run paper mode, start with small clips, review fills, verify cancellations, and keep daily loss limits conservative.

Do Not Deposit Into Monatise

Current policy

Monatise should not collect user trading deposits, hold customer crypto, or promise withdrawals from an internal balance. That would require a much heavier compliance, custody, reconciliation, and fraud-control program.
