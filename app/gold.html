Commodity Risk Checks

Before arm
  1. Check the event calendar Rates, inflation, payrolls, crude inventory reports, OPEC headlines, and geopolitical shocks can invalidate a range.
  2. Widen or pause before news Gold and oil can whip through tight grids. Reduce order count or stop the desk before high-impact releases.
  3. Use idle reserve Keep 50% or more idle until the strategy has clean fills and controlled drawdown over several sessions.
  4. Stop when the range breaks A grid is not a rescue plan. If price leaves the thesis, cancel orders and re-map from the new structure.
