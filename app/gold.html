Why gold fits the grid conversation

Gold often rotates around macro levels, but it can break violently when rates, inflation, or crisis flows move.

Monatise should treat XAU as a planning-first instrument: build range maps, size conservatively, and only route live orders when the connected venue explicitly reports a tradable gold market.

Routing rule

Do not fake gold execution.

  • XAU can be watched and planned in Monatise
  • Live orders require venue-supported XAU routing
  • If XAU is unavailable, keep it as strategy preview
  • Never label simulated XAU orders as live exchange orders

Gold-Specific Risk Checks

Before arm
  1. Check the event calendar Rates decisions, inflation data, payrolls, central-bank commentary, and crisis headlines can invalidate a range.
  2. Widen or pause before news Gold can whip through tight grids. Reduce order count or stop the desk before high-impact releases.
  3. Use idle reserve Keep 50% or more idle until the strategy has clean fills and controlled drawdown over several sessions.
  4. Stop when the range breaks A grid is not a rescue plan. If price leaves the thesis, cancel orders and re-map from the new structure.
