Platform thesis

Monatise is built for disciplined market participation, not prediction theatre.

The desk plans inventory-aware grid orders, places liquidity around live market marks, reconciles exchange fills, and keeps the operator focused on exposure, sync, and risk state.

Operating Model

Desk architecture
01 Plan

Generate bid and ask levels around the active mark with inventory skew controls.

02 Gate

Check account value, notional limits, mark shocks, exposure, and daily loss caps.

03 Execute

Submit small clipped orders only when the live gates and user credentials are ready.

04 Reconcile

Poll exchange fills, map them back to local orders, and refresh the grid from real state.

Why It Exists

Liquidity over impulse

Most trading interfaces over-celebrate entry calls. Monatise is designed around repeatable liquidity placement, risk ceilings, and the operational truth that the exchange state is the source of record.
